Rules of the Game

Enter in the number of dice and the number of sides for the dice.

You earn points in two ways:

  1. You earn points by rolling prime numbers.
  2. If the sum of your roll is a prime number you will get bonus points equal to the sum of your roll.

You will loose points for each 1 you roll.
